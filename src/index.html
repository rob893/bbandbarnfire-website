About

Origin and Formation

Formed in 2022 in Marietta, GA, Know Ones emerged from the vision of Brandon Barker, who sought to assemble a group of wildly talented and chaotic individuals. Together with Charles Mowery on drums, Abe Powell on bass guitar, and Rob Herber on lead guitar, the band was born.

Musical Style and Influences

The band has coined the term "y'allternative" to describe their eclectic sound, which blends elements of pop, rock, pop punk, and country. Drawing inspiration from artists like Post Malone, A Day To Remember, Queens of the Stone Age, Foo Fighters, Hardy, and Morgan Wallen, their music offers a unique fusion that is both familiar and refreshingly innovative.

Releases and Achievements

Know Ones has released five singles, all available on major streaming platforms. They have also produced two professional music videos and are actively booking shows to share their music with a wider audience.

Performances and Tours

The band has performed live several times, bringing their unique sound to various venues. They are currently focused on booking larger gigs in Atlanta and the surrounding areas, equipped with their own professional gear. While they haven't embarked on a tour yet, they are eager to be part of a major tour soon.

Band Vision and Goals

The band's mission is to create a unique blend of their favorite genres, delivering music that resonates emotionally and gets people moving. Looking ahead, they aim to return to the studio to write and record a new album and aspire to tour internationally, sharing their distinctive sound with the world.

Unique Elements

What sets Know Ones apart is their innovative fusion of country, pop, pop punk, and folk music, combined with funky time signatures and memorable melodies. Their sound is a testament to their ability to seamlessly blend diverse musical influences into something uniquely their own.
